The night I lost my santuary

3350 Anon

 
                As time went on, the lights got out
All of a sudden. I heard a trembling shout
Of someone I couldn't know
I couldn't know if the would be my friend or foe
With watery eyes. I turned to my right
I missed someone, there was none to fight!
I turned to my left and found a lack
On God! There was none to itch my back.
The small blue light got out
But the flight of memories of home took off
In my small, heavy, shocked mind
Someone like them, would I ever find?
I was sent there for glory, respect and fame,
But my life without you would not be same
At the dead of night. I fell a strange fear
But there was none for giving me care
They say they would turn boys into men
But there was a lot of time left.
I was only ten "A ranked officer you will be at last"
But, freedom and peace, the things- I seek first
Belts and ties were hanging in the cupboard
But my dreams and hopes seemed to go far and far
Now I am working for my country
But I realized. I lost my sanctuary.
