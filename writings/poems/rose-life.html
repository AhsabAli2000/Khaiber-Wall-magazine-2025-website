Rose coloured life

3198 Rafat

 
                One day I was hoping for
A rose coloured life!
A white canvas in my hand
Splashing colour on my rose
An aged shaky brush
Working with colours!
"Will this work?" I questioned.
But I still worked
To make a successful one.
My efforts never stopped
Some old colours I got
"Whose are these?" I asked myself.
There is no time
I dived into colours
The deepest red on the canvas
But satisfaction was not there
I mined a little white No!
Something Lifeless!
I spread a bit orange
There's the sunrise
I never stopped
But what I made!
No rose! No rose!
An unknown follicle!
My query mind,
Can a blind man create colour?
I seek for God's boon
Let's not make me sad soon
Oh! A dream it was

A rose coloured dream
Just like the rose
On my table
"Wait! Can it be?'
Was my last question.
