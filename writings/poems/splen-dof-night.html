Splendor of night

3307 Imam

 
                The night is so calm and quiet.
Little stars are shining bright
Crisping voice of the gentle breeze
Passing the innocent lives soft and clean
The queen of the flow splashes a silver glow
On the beautiful world that rest and sleep below
Dreams fly freely on small silent wings
In the dark and peace the nighttime brings
Crickets sing their lullaby in little free hearts
Underneath the mighty sky that can't be apart
Nighttime wraps the whole world in love and grace
Bringing peace and calm to every place
