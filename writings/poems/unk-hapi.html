Unknown Happiness

3205 Tahmid

 
                Feast was going on
As well the discussion
Scrumptious was the supper's preparation!
At once, pin drop silence!
None could continue more.
Dad did hold our attention
"Lingering for the bus with restless eyes, he said.
The boy stretches his hand
Too long sleeves of his shirt,'
Dad asked for the valid cause.
'Beggars must not be choosers! the answer was
there but unheard
Only a stacks, the boy desired.
The hypnotic eyes trapped my dad.
Foggy queries were no more!
Dad dared to ignore.
A man felt for the boy.
The Omnipotent showered the joy.
Bridge was built.
The boy conveyed thanks
Not in words.
Dad read more than that
The boy's eyes shared
Breeze felt celestial
Words not essential
"Only save your apologies
Agony will never seize?'
